Capstone Design Project: Moment

Moment is an automated conference calling system. It has two cameras which can rotate a continuous 360 degrees independipendently. Using a microphone array and computer vision AI it can detect 'iteresting' people (ie. people talking or people doing something interesting). The system then moves the nearest camera to that person and streams from that camera.
I developed the mechanical design, wrote the AI/Computer vision, wrote mapping software and wired the system.
